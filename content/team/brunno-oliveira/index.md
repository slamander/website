**Bio:**

My research focuses on understanding the ecological and evolutionary mechanisms that jointly shape the distribution of life on Earth, with the ultimate goal to predict how ongoing environmental change can impact biodiversity and ecosystem functioning, including ecosystem services beneficial for humans. I do so using innovative methods and tools that allow me to manipulate and analyze large datasets on species distributions, traits, phylogenies, and remote sensing generated variables. My research is mostly computational, and I am an avid Rlogo user.

Currently I am a postdoc based at the Centre de Synthèse et d’Analyse sur la Biodiversité (CESAB) – Fondation pour la Recherche sur la Biodiversité (FRB), France – working in the BIOSHIFTS project.
